Immaculate grounds, rolling hills, and undulating fast greens combine to create a memorable experience with each round of golf.

Designed with
everyone in mind.

Due to the shorter yardage length, Benona Shores Golf Club presents a great opportunity for golfers of all levels. Whether you're just starting off in the game or have been playing for an entire lifetime, Benona Shores is an environment where everyone feels welcomed. With 3 tee marker options for each hole, the level of challenge for the round is up to the individual.
