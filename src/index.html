Discover a Hidden Gem.

Located just off the coast of Lake Michigan, Benona Shores is an Executive Golf Course nestled among a vibrant landscape of pristine rolling hills and gentle lake breezes.

Referred to as a "Hidden Gem," this immaculate 4380-yard, par 60 golf course consists of challenging, undulating terrain with smooth, fast greens. It presents a true test for the most experienced golfers while featuring a short, walkable course that is equally enjoyable for beginners. The average round of play is just under 3 hours.
